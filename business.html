F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of insurance do you offer?

We offer comprehensive insurance solutions including Motor, Home, Health, Life, Travel, and SME Business insurance. All policies are tailored to the Cyprus market with competitive rates and excellent coverage.

How quickly can I get a quote?

You'll receive a personalized quote within 10 minutes of contacting me. I respond to all inquiries immediately – no waiting, no automated systems, just direct personal service.

What happens if I need to make a claim?

Contact me immediately and I'll handle everything. We begin processing your claim right away and I personally guide you through each step. Resolution time depends on the insurer and documentation required. You'll have direct access to me throughout the entire process.

Do you offer 24/7 roadside assistance?

Yes! All motor insurance policies include 24/7 roadside assistance across Cyprus. Whether it's a breakdown, flat tire, or accident, help is always available.

Can I customize my insurance coverage?

Absolutely! We work together to choose the right excess and coverage terms for your needs and budget. You pay for what you need – no unnecessary extras.

Do you serve clients outside of Limassol?

Yes! While based in Limassol, I serve clients across all of Cyprus including Nicosia, Larnaca, Paphos, and Famagusta. Remote consultations available via phone, WhatsApp, or video call.
