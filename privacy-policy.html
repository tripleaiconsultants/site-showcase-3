1. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact data: name, email address, phone number
  • Request data: insurance type of interest, message content
  • Technical data: security logs (e.g. IP address, browser type) collected automatically for site security purposes

Important: Please do not send sensitive data (e.g. health details) via the contact form or WhatsApp. If sensitive information is required for your insurance request, a human advisor will guide you through a secure channel.

2. Why We Process Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your quote or appointment requests
  • To contact you regarding your enquiry
  • To maintain site security and prevent abuse
  • For marketing communications (only with your separate, explicit consent)

3. Legal Bases

We rely on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent: When you submit a contact form or subscribe to marketing communications, you provide your consent for the processing of your data for the stated purpose.
  • Pre-contractual measures: Processing necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into an insurance contract (e.g. preparing a quote).
  • Legitimate interest: Processing necessary for our legitimate interests, such as maintaining site security and improving our services, provided these interests are not overridden by your rights.

4. Who Receives Your Data

Your personal data may be shared with the following recipients:

  • IT and hosting providers: Third-party service providers who assist with website hosting, maintenance, and security.
  • Insurance companies: Your data may be shared with insurance companies solely for the purpose of obtaining quotes or processing claims, and only with your knowledge and for the purpose you have requested.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Requests and quotes: up to 24 months after the last communication
  • Claims-related data: as required by applicable law and regulation
  • Marketing data: until you withdraw your consent or 36 months of inactivity, whichever comes first

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to restriction: You can ask us to restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time via Cookie Settings.
